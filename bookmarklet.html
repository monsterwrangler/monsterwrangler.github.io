Custom Icons for BostonPogoMap

Once here, you need to bookmark this page:

  • On Safari, tap the Share icon, then "Add Bookmark".
  • On Chrome, tap the three dots in the upper right, then the star.

Next, edit the bookmark:

  • On Safari, tap the Bookmark icon, then "Edit" in the lower right, then the BostonPogoMap Icons bookmark.
  • On Chrome, tap the three dots in the upper right, then "Bookmarks", then the three dots to the right of the "BostonPogoMap Icons", then "Edit".

Remove everything at the start of the bookmark's URL, up to and including the "?#". You'll be left with a URL that starts with "javascript:".

Once you've edited your bookmark:

  1. Go to BostonPogoMap.com
  2. Open your bookmarks.
  3. Tap your BostonPogoMap Icons bookmark.

BostonPogoMap.com should reload, magically with the icons restored! If ever they vanish again, you should be able to repeat the three steps above to recover them.
